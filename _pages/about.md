Hi! I am Kartik. I am a 4th-Year undergraduate computer science engineering student from Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani. I am broadly interested in Machine Learning and Deep Learning, particularly in Computer Vision, and applications of ML in finance.

I am currently working as a research assistant at The Advanced Data Analytics and Parallel Technologies (ADAPT) Lab , BITS Pilani on estimating crop yield using the fusion of Landsat and Sentinel satellite data. 

Alongside, I am working as a research assistant at Central Electronics Engineering Research Institute (CSIR-CEERI), Pilani on instance segmentation framework to recognize highly cluttered instances of contraband items. I previously worked on optimizing portfolios using machine learning.

I have interned at Kizora Software Private Limited, a solution provider on cloud platform focused on data processing and analytics, where i worked on building a website to provide optimized routes (with any number of stoppages) to travel from one place to another (through any mode of travel).

In my free time, I am either at gym, or binging tv shows/animes. I love travelling, covering almost each and every state in India. I have travelled around 20 states in India, and have been to Singapore and Malayasia. I am a big fan of Weekend and Travis Scott :)
