Kartik Sharma

I'm a first-year MSR student at Carnegie Mellon University working on multimodal perception and embodied AI, with a current focus on 3D/4D reconstruction from video and hand-object interaction. I'm advised by Prof. Laszlo A. Jeni. Previously, I built multilingual safety filters and on-device vision-language models at Samsung R&D, shipping compact models to flagship devices. I earned a dual degree in Computer Science & Economics from BITS Pilani.

I'm especially interested in collaborations around video-based 3D/4D understanding, hand-object interaction, embodied multimodal agents, and safe deployment of vision-language-action systems.
